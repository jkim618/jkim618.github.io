Path

I led the design of the dashboard for the USI PATH platform, which serves as a central hub for consolidating both external-facing sales presentations and internal tools for producers.

Role

Lead UX Designer

Duration

August 2023 - January 2024

Team

Product Manager, Engineers, QA, 2 other Designers

Context

In the dynamic landscape of risk management and health and wellness, the USI PATH platform emerged as a game-changer, promising to Prevent, Abate, Uncover treasures, and improve Health for both employees and organizations.

Problem

After receiving the initial concept design from stakeholders, our UX team identified critical issues. The design lacked intuitive navigation, a clear information hierarchy, and a cohesive user flow, posing challenges for users in comprehending and navigating the platform effectively.

Final Solution

The redesigned dashboard introduces a clear information hierarchy, intuitive navigation, and a cohesive user flow, enhancing the platform's usability. These improvements aim to provide a more streamlined and user-friendly experience for PATH platform users.

Research and Insight

Our team conducted several stakeholder meetings to gather crucial insights, shaping the redesign process to align with business objectives and user needs effectively.

Solution Matrix Graph Emphasis: Stakeholders highlighted the critical importance of the solution matrix graph as a key element for the platform.

Incorporation of 5 Lines of Coverage: Stakeholders highlighted the requirement for incorporating 5 lines of coverage within a specific industry and subvertical.

Favorites Feature: The business sought to implement a favorites feature that enables clients to save specific solutions and vendors for easy access and reference.

Goal

Our goal was to improve the platform's usability, ensuring that users could easily navigate and comprehend the information presented to them.

Enhance Navigation: Improve the platform's navigation to allow users to move between sections seamlessly and access features efficiently.

Clarify Information Hierarchy: Establish a clear and logical arrangement of content to help users easily find and understand relevant information.

Create a Cohesive User Flow: Develop a consistent and intuitive flow throughout the dashboard to guide users through their tasks and interactions smoothly.

Facilitate Data Exploration: Enable users to explore and analyze data effectively by providing robust filtering options, allowing them to refine their view and focus on specific information relevant to their needs.

Design Decision 1: Reorganizing Dashboard Sections for Improved Usability

Initially, the dashboard featured a long, scrollable page that combined "Loss Impact" and "Solution Matrix" into a single section. To enhance the user experience and reduce cognitive overload, I redesigned the layout to implement a navigation system that separated these two sections. This update allows users to effortlessly switch between sections using distinct call-to-action buttons, promoting a more intuitive and streamlined experience. By dividing the "Loss Impact" and "Solution Matrix" sections into two separate pages, we reduced the amount of content that needed to be loaded at once, which improved the platform's performance and enhanced content organization.

Design Decision 2: Use of Filter Chips

To provide users with more control over the displayed data, we implemented filter chips. These filter chips allow users to refine the displayed graph and table by selecting specific loss drivers. This granular filtering capability enables users to focus on relevant information

Outcomes

Influenced product roadmap: The redesigned onboarding process is expected to influence the future direction of Sequeer, enhancing user control and security during app navigation and usage.

Pivot direction based on research: Our research gave us confidence to propose to Sequeer a pivot from phone number signup to a more user-centric and flexible signup and login experience, providing greater choice and adaptability for users.

Saved onboarding time: Rudimentary user testing indicates that our redesigned onboarding process can help users save time when setting up their Sequeer accounts and exploring the app's features.
