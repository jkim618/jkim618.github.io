Reducing Cognitive Overload

To streamline navigation and reduce visual load, I split the previously combined layout into two dedicated pages. Stakeholders confirmed that while the 'Loss Impact' chart was helpful, the 'Solution Matrix' deserved a focused space to support decision-making and scalability.

Before

  • All content on one page increased cognitive load
  • "View Solution Matrix" only scrolled to bottom
  • Performance lag due to heavy content

After

  • Split into two pages: Loss Impact & Solution Matrix
  • "View Solution Matrix" now navigates to a dedicated view
  • Improved clarity, speed, and future scalability

This change was driven by feedback during stakeholder sessions and tested internally for performance gains and clearer user journeys.
