Appian Case Management

I led the design of citizen portal landing page for Appian Constituent Case Management to enhance the user experience for citizens as well as creating the case creation process.

Role

UX Designer

Duration

May 2022 - July 2022

Team

Product Manager, Engineers, QA

Context

Constituent Case Management involves handling cases related to citizens at the state and local government level. It includes the intake, processing, and recording of activities for cases, which can range from investigations to approvals. This workflow allows users to submit cases on behalf of citizens or for citizens to submit cases themselves.

Problem

Feedback from stakeholders and partners highlighted a key challenge: the current citizen-facing experience lacks clear guidance. Upon navigating to the agency's welcome page, citizens may not immediately recognize the need to 'create a case' and what that exactly entails. This lack of clarity results in users not having enough context to take appropriate actions.

Final Solution

The redesign aims to provide a more guided user experience with clearer context. It includes suggested case types with explanations, a search feature for quick case identification, helpful documentation and resources, and the ability for users to create an account and register.

User Flow

I developed a user flow illustrating how citizens can navigate the new portal to create and submit a case.

Final Designs

Goal

Our goal was to improve the platform's usability, ensuring that users could easily navigate and comprehend the information presented to them.

Enhance Navigation: Improve the platform's navigation to allow users to move between sections seamlessly and access features efficiently.

Clarify Information Hierarchy: Establish a clear and logical arrangement of content to help users easily find and understand relevant information.

Create a Cohesive User Flow: Develop a consistent and intuitive flow throughout the dashboard to guide users through their tasks and interactions smoothly.

Facilitate Data Exploration: Enable users to explore and analyze data effectively by providing robust filtering options, allowing them to refine their view and focus on specific information relevant to their needs.

Iterate, iterate, iterate

I iterated on the dashboard's solution matrix to enhance the user experience. The current design features color-coded keys that improve information organization and efficiency. By optimizing text density in the table and its preview, the design makes effective use of UI real estate, ensuring a clean and concise presentation of data.

Outcomes

Alignment with Business Objectives: The redesigned onboarding process is expected to influence the future direction of Sequeer, enhancing user control and security during app navigation and usage.

Pivot direction based on research: Our research gave us confidence to propose to Sequeer a pivot from phone number signup to a more user-centric and flexible signup and login experience, providing greater choice and adaptability for users.

Saved onboarding time: Rudimentary user testing indicates that our redesigned onboarding process can help users save time when setting up their Sequeer accounts and exploring the app's features.
