Who am I as a designer?

I'm Jane Kim, a first-generation Korean American Product Designer from the Northern Virginia area with 3 years of experience in UX and Product Design and 2 years of experience in software engineering.

I thrive on collaboration, especially during brainstorming sessions, where I connect with people to uncover their needs and craft innovative solutions.

I’ve worked on diverse projects, from designing Case Management solutions at Appian Corporation to leading UX initiatives with ViaVia, Sequeer, and Terrascope. These projects included fashion software, AI-driven real estate tools, and mobile search engines, showcasing my adaptability across domains.

I'm always open to great opportunities. Please don't hesitate to reach out— I'm happy to chat about work or anything else!
